Privacy Policy for Sri Lanka Electricity Bill Calculator Extension

Effective Date: February 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sri Lanka Electricity Bill Calculator extension ("Extension") collects, uses, and protects user data. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

2. Data Collection

No Personal Data Collected: The Extension does not collect any personal or sensitive information. All data, such as calculation history, is stored locally on your device.

Local Processing: Any data used by the Extension is processed locally and solely for the purpose of accurately calculating your electricity bill using the latest tariff rates.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. All data remains private and under your control.

4. Data Storage

Local Storage Only: The Extension stores data only on your device. There is no data synchronization or uploading to external servers, ensuring that your information remains secure and private.

5. Third-Party Resources

Trusted Libraries: The Extension uses trusted third-party resources (for example, Font Awesome for icons) strictly for enhancing the user interface. These resources do not collect or store any personal data.
